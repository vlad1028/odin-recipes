Classic Caesar Salad

Photograph By Isa Zapata, Food Styling By Judy Kim, Prop Styling By Stephanie De Luca

Recipe by sarahcrites

Description

This Caesar salad is the same recipe we made at a very fancy restaurant while I was in college. The best part of the job was making tableside dishes, including this Caesar salad which is the best I've ever had.

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 of lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Salad

  • 1/2 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons croutons

Steps

Make the Dressing

  1. Mash anchovy fillets and garlic in a large salad bowl
  2. Add lemon juice, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, egg yolk and Worcestershire sauce
  3. Whisk until smooth and creamy; gradually stream in olive oil while whisking constantly
  4. Season with salt and black pepper

Make Salad

  1. Gently mix romaine lettuce and Parmesan cheese into dressing until thoroughly coated
  2. Serve salad topped with croutons
