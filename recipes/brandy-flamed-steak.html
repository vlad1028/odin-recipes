Brandy Flamed Peppercorn Steak

Recipe by TFRIESEN

Description

A five-star recipe for peppercorn steak cooked in butter, garlic, and wine, and then set on fire! Crushed peppercorns, brandy, and a cream sauce give this an extra classy taste.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons crushed black peppercorns
  • 2 (8-ounce) beef top sirloin steaks
  • 1 tablespoon lemon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 5 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 3 tablespoons brandy
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 shallot, chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar (Optional)

Steps

    1. Press crushed peppercorns into both sides of each steak.
    2. Sprinkle with lemon pepper and salt.
    1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Stir in garlic and wine and cook for 1 minute
    3. Arrange steaks in pan and cook until desired doneness, about 5 minutes per side.
    4. Reduce heat to low.
    1. Pour brandy onto steaks and carefully light with a match. Let the flames burn off.
    2. Sprinkle green onion and shallot around steaks, and circle steaks with cream.
    3. Cook, stirring sauce, until hot.
    4. Transfer steaks to plates.
    5. Stir sugar into sauce and then spoon over meat
