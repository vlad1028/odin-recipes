Banana Pancakes

Recipe by ADDEAN1

Description

Wake up on the right side of the bed with a stack of sweet, cozy, and simple banana pancakes. This top-rated banana pancake recipe is easy to make and it comes together in just 15 minutes, so you don't have to wake up early to enjoy a satisfying breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed

Steps

  1. Combine flour, white sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Mix together egg, milk, vegetable oil, and bananas in a second bowl.
  3. Stir flour mixture into banana mixture; batter will be slightly lumpy.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  6. Cook until pancakes are golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes per side.
  7. Serve hot.
